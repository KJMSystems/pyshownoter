What is Shownoter

Shownoter checks your chat or text for links and returns them to you to help create "shownotes".

Shownoter will help you find links from group chats, podcasts interviews, and website articles.

**Shownoter is still in beta. Please do not rely on Shownoter to preserve shownotes data.**
